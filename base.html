About socality

Founded in 2014, Socality is a creative community that exists to inspire people from all walks of life to create things that make a difference. Socality began with the simple goal of connecting creatives online and in person using social media and live events. Over the years this has grown into a vision that includes local community events, workshops, creative camps, online content and storytelling, and collective initiatives designed to engage the community at large around projects and organizations.

In Socality, they often hold one or two house parties at 7 p.m. on the weekends once a month. You can make new people at that party who share your interests while also getting your heat on with the guest dj's selection of enticing music.Additionally, they will have a Halloween party in November on the evening of the 28th; if you have the opportunity, attend there.
